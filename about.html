logo.

was started by a 16-year-old high school student who had a passion for graphic design and positive messages.

what began as an interesting hobby at the age of 14 quickly sprouted into a pursuit of creativity. with numerous opportunities, Jude House was able to express his creative urge in volunteer positions; after over a year of volunteering, he had developed his creative mind and marketing knowledge to a point where he was ready to take it to next step....

through partnership with Nott Shots, Jude was able to reach real cliental with real needs. this partnership allowed him to grow knowledge in the field of marketing and strategy, as well as real-world work experience.

in further pursuit of developing his creative mind and knowledge in the field of design, Jude found himself at an internship position with Kelp; an agency which provides web design/development, business/brand strategy, content creation, and graphical marketing.

to this day, Jude continues to work closely with Nott Shots and further develop his creative arsenal and work experience.
